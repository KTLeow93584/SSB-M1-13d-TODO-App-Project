Feeling like your life is too organized and need some distraction? Say no more!

Become perpetually distracted and never be productive in a single day. The world's #1 to-do manager app with a procrastinating twist!

Over 100 million+ users trust us with sating their desire to slack off!

Overview

1. Click on the custom task button to add a new task!

2. Alternatively, you can also hit the "Distract Me!" to receive a random distracting video ranging from cute animal videos to memes!

3. Tasks can be modified via the icon under the "Actions" column!

4. Getting tired of certain tasks sitting rent-free in your head? Look for the icon under the "Actions" column to remove them!

5. Profit! Watch your entire day drift away as the content consumes your free time.

6. Feel free to register a free account to allow your board's tasks to be saved. Note that the data is currently only saved locally and since this is just a mock website, DO NOT use your actual credentials when creating a new account.
